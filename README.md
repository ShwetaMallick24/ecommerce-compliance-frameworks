## Overview

This repository documents the governance frameworks I designed and implemented to scale regulated product categories on major e-commerce marketplaces — without sacrificing trust or safety.

At TikTok Shop, I built compliance infrastructure from scratch for categories like medical devices, supplements, and cosmetics. At Amazon, I refined existing safeguards to help sellers move faster while staying compliant. The frameworks here reflect real systems that drove measurable outcomes.

## Core Philosophy

> *"Compliance isn't just about avoiding penalties — it determines who gets seen, who gets trusted, and ultimately who wins."*

Most marketplace teams treat compliance as a blocker. I designed systems where **compliance becomes a growth lever** — a way for sellers to differentiate, buyers to trust, and platforms to expand faster.

The key insight: rules are filters. Sellers who understand them deeply can turn compliance into competitive advantage.
